# ALPHA DROP — Ad Campaign Content Generation Prompt

Copy and paste everything below into ChatGPT, Claude, or any content LLM:

---

You are an elite performance marketing copywriter specializing in SaaS, e-commerce tools, and TikTok Shop. You're creating a full ad campaign for **ALPHA DROP** — a product intelligence engine that identifies viral TikTok Shop products BEFORE they explode using a proprietary "Alpha Score" algorithm.

## PRODUCT DETAILS

**What it is:** ALPHA DROP is a Bloomberg Terminal-style dark-themed web app that tracks real TikTok Shop products in real-time. It uses a proprietary 7-factor Alpha Score (0-100) to predict which products will go viral — analyzing velocity, creator adoption, engagement quality, hook strength, market expansion, saturation risk, and repeatability.

**Key features:**
- Real-time product intelligence from TikTok Shop (271+ products tracked live)
- Proprietary Alpha Score algorithm that identifies products in the "accumulation phase" — before they blow up
- Hook Intelligence System — shows which video hooks and emotional triggers are working right now
- Cross-Platform Market Validation — TikTok vs Amazon vs Google Trends demand gaps (arbitrage opportunities)
- TikTok Affiliate marketplace — find creators by niche, see commission rates, contact info
- Real-time alerts when products enter the "EXPLOSIVE" zone
- CSV exports, historical tracking, API access

**Pricing:**
- Scout: $79/mo (50 products, basic Alpha Score)
- Hunter: $199/mo (500 products, full analytics, hook intelligence, affiliate data) — MOST POPULAR
- Predator: $499/mo (unlimited, API, team seats, full affiliate tools, Google Trends)
- Affiliate Pro Add-on: +$99/mo (full affiliate marketplace for Hunter users, included in Predator)

**Competitors & positioning:** Kalodata charges $199-$799/mo for LESS functionality. Minea is $49-$199 but basic. PiPiADS is $77-$263 for ad spy only. ALPHA DROP combines ALL of these into one platform at a competitive price with unique features (Alpha Score, hook intelligence, affiliate marketplace) that NO competitor has.

**Target audiences:**
1. TikTok Shop sellers looking for winning products to sell
2. Dropshippers and e-commerce entrepreneurs
3. TikTok content creators wanting to find high-commission affiliate products
4. Agencies managing TikTok Shop brands for clients

**Brand voice:** Confident, data-driven, urgent but not desperate. Mix of aggressive hustle energy ("your competitors found this product yesterday"), professional credibility ("powered by real-time behavioral signals"), and FOMO ("this product had an Alpha Score of 89 three days before it did $200K in sales"). Think Wall Street meets TikTok — sharp, fast, money-focused.

**CTAs to rotate across ads:**
- Sign up for free trial
- Subscribe now (drive to paid)
- Join the beta waitlist (founding members get 25% off for life)
- Book a demo

---

## YOUR TASK

Generate a COMPLETE ad campaign with content for ALL of the following platforms. For each platform, create **3 ad variations** (one for each tone: aggressive/hustle, data-driven/professional, FOMO-heavy). Each variation should have a version targeting sellers, one targeting creators/affiliates, and one targeting agencies. That's 9 pieces per platform.

### PLATFORM 1: TikTok / Instagram Reels (Short-Form Video Scripts)
- 15-30 second scripts
- Include: hook (first 2 seconds), body, CTA
- Written as spoken word with stage directions [B-ROLL: show dashboard], [CUT TO: pricing], etc.
- Make the hooks scroll-stopping — these compete with entertainment content
- Reference specific product examples (make up realistic TikTok Shop product scenarios)

### PLATFORM 2: Facebook / Instagram (Static + Carousel Ads)
- Primary text (above the fold, 125 chars visible)
- Headline (40 chars max)
- Description (30 chars max)
- For carousels: 5 slides with headline + description each
- Include suggested image/visual direction for each

### PLATFORM 3: Twitter/X (Text Ads + Threads)
- Single tweet ads (280 chars)
- Thread format (5-7 tweets telling a story)
- Quote-tweet style ("I found a product with a 94 Alpha Score on Monday. By Thursday it had done $180K. Here's how:")
- Use the degen/hustle Twitter tone that performs well in e-commerce spaces

### PLATFORM 4: YouTube (Pre-Roll + Mid-Roll Scripts)
- 15-second non-skippable pre-roll script
- 30-second skippable pre-roll (hook must land in first 5 seconds)
- 60-second mid-roll script (more educational, builds credibility)
- Include visual direction and pacing notes

### PLATFORM 5: Google Ads (Search + Display)
- 5 responsive search ad variations (30-char headlines x3, 90-char descriptions x2)
- Target keywords to bid on (include competitor keywords)
- 2 display ad copy variations (short headline + description)
- Include negative keywords to exclude

---

## FORMATTING RULES
- Label every piece clearly: [PLATFORM] > [VARIATION] > [AUDIENCE]
- Bold the hooks/headlines
- Include estimated read/watch time
- Add a "WHY THIS WORKS" note under each piece explaining the psychology
- End with a section called "A/B TESTING RECOMMENDATIONS" suggesting which variations to test first and why

---

## IMPORTANT NOTES
- Never say "AI-powered" — position it as a proprietary algorithm built by product researchers
- Use specific numbers and scenarios to build credibility (e.g., "271 products tracked", "7-factor algorithm", "$499/mo Predator plan")
- Reference the pain point: "By the time you see a product trending on your FYP, it's already too late. The margins are gone. The market is saturated."
- The Alpha Score is the hero — it's the thing that makes ALPHA DROP different from everything else
- For affiliate-focused ads, emphasize: finding high-commission products, connecting with niche creators, and the earnings potential
- For agency-focused ads, emphasize: team seats, API access, managing multiple brands, client reporting
